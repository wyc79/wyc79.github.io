My contributions included scripting, designing and executing comprehensive unit tests to validate core functionality, remove redundant code, and ensure the package remained lightweight and maintainable. I worked closely with teammates through regular check-ins, code reviews, and debugging sessions, integrating individual modules into a cohesive, production-ready tool.
