Why为什么做

I wanted to practice translating a stylized game prop into a clean, manufacturable CAD model—especially handling asymmetry, layered details, and tight proportion matching from reference.我想练习把一个风格化的游戏道具转化为干净、可制造的 CAD 模型，尤其是处理不对称结构、层叠细节，以及严格对照参考图匹配比例。

This was my final project for EAS 143: Introduction to Solid Modeling (University of Rochester, Spring 2021), where the goal was to build accurate parts and assemblies using parametric workflows.这是我在 EAS 143：实体建模导论（罗切斯特大学，2021 年春季）的期末项目，目标是运用参数化工作流构建精确的零件与装配体。

What做了什么

A detailed 3D CAD replication of the Aegis Sword from Xenoblade Chronicles 2, modeled in Onshape with an emphasis on readable silhouette, precise detailing, and assembly-ready structure.这是对 Xenoblade Chronicles 2 中 Aegis Sword 的一次精细三维 CAD 复刻，在 Onshape 中建模，注重清晰的剪影轮廓、精准的细节刻画，以及便于装配的结构。

How如何实现

Workflow工作流程

  • Reference breakdown: Collected screenshots and orthographic-ish angles, then identified key proportions (blade length, guard width, handle thickness) to lock the silhouette early.
  • 拆解参考：收集截图与接近正交视角的角度图，找出关键比例（剑身长度、护手宽度、剑柄粗细），从而尽早确定剪影轮廓。
  • Modular parts: Split the sword into repeatable subcomponents (blade core, guard, handle, emblem/ornament), so edits wouldn’t cascade across the whole model.
  • 模块化零件：将剑拆分为可复用的子部件（剑身主体、护手、剑柄、纹章/装饰件），这样一处修改不会牵动整个模型。
  • Parametric feature tree: Used sketches + extrudes/fillets/patterns with named dimensions to keep the model editable during iteration.
  • 参数化特征树：使用草图配合拉伸、圆角、阵列等特征，并为尺寸命名，使模型在迭代过程中始终可编辑。

Design & detailing设计与细节

  • Asymmetry handling: Established a consistent datum setup and only mirrored where the design truly repeats; the rest was modeled intentionally off-axis to match the original.
  • 处理不对称：建立一套统一的基准，仅在设计确实重复之处使用镜像；其余部分刻意偏离轴线建模，以贴合原作。
  • Clean edges & readability: Tuned bevels/fillets to avoid “too sharp” transitions while keeping crisp highlights on the main forms.
  • 干净的边缘与可读性：调整倒角与圆角，避免过于生硬的过渡，同时在主要形体上保留清晰的高光。
  • Assembly mindset: Modeled as an assembly with part separation in mind (useful for future export, printing, or game-ready retopology workflows).
  • 装配思维：以装配体的方式建模，并考虑零件的分件（便于日后导出、打印，或面向游戏的重拓扑流程）。

Result成果

The final model reproduces the sword’s layered structure and distinctive asymmetry, and the modular setup made it straightforward to refine proportions and surface details late in the process.最终模型还原了这把剑的层叠结构与鲜明的不对称感，而模块化的搭建方式让我在流程后期也能轻松打磨比例与表面细节。
