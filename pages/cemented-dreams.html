What做了什么

Cemented Dreams is a third-person action game set inside a parasite-infested building. Players navigate an infected structure where a mechanical Hive grows through the architecture, creating traversal paths and combat spaces.Cemented Dreams 是一款第三人称动作游戏，故事发生在一座被寄生体侵蚀的建筑内部。玩家在受感染的结构中穿行，一座机械 Hive 在建筑中不断生长，形成位移路径与战斗空间。

I worked primarily on combat design, gameplay programming, and level design, building core movement and combat mechanics while coordinating closely with artists and producers.我主要负责战斗设计、玩法编程与关卡设计，构建核心的移动与战斗机制，同时与美术和制作人密切协作。

Gameplay systems were implemented using a combination of Unreal Engine C++ gameplay code and Blueprint-driven iteration.玩法系统通过 Unreal Engine 的 C++ 玩法代码与 Blueprint 驱动的迭代相结合来实现。

How如何实现

Combat Design + Gameplay Engineering战斗设计 + 玩法工程

Level Design - Hive Simulation关卡设计：Hive 模拟

I designed a level centered around a parasitic Hive structure growing inside a building, creating traversal paths and combat arenas through environmental transformation.我设计了一个以寄生 Hive 结构在建筑内部生长为核心的关卡，通过环境的变形创造出位移路径与战斗场地。

  • The central Hive structure grows into the building while cables from the structure attempt to strangle it.
  • 中央的 Hive 结构向建筑内部生长，而建筑的缆索则试图将其绞杀。
  • At cable contact points, the Hive generates hexagonal platforms that players can traverse.
  • 在缆索的接触点，Hive 会生成供玩家穿行的六边形平台。
  • Entrances into the Hive are torn open by structural cables, creating layered vertical gameplay spaces.
  • 进入 Hive 的入口被结构缆索撕开，形成层叠的垂直玩法空间。
  • A critical injection point forms around a cable that pierces the Hive wall, granting players the grapple ability.
  • 在一条穿透 Hive 墙体的缆索周围形成了关键的注入点，赋予玩家钩爪能力。

This level structure links narrative worldbuilding with traversal mechanics, turning the parasite's growth into both a visual motif and a gameplay system.这一关卡结构将叙事世界观与位移机制相连接，把寄生体的生长同时转化为视觉母题与玩法系统。
