Cemented Dreams — Gameplay Systems

Role: Combat Designer · Gameplay Engineer · Level Designer
Tools: Unreal Engine 5, C++, Blueprint

What

Cemented Dreams is a third-person action game set inside a parasite-infested building. Players navigate an infected structure where a mechanical Hive grows through the architecture, creating traversal paths and combat spaces.

I worked primarily on combat design, gameplay programming, and level design, building core movement and combat mechanics while coordinating closely with artists and producers.

Gameplay systems were implemented using a combination of Unreal Engine C++ gameplay code and Blueprint-driven iteration.

Main Achievements

  • Designed and implemented core combat movement mechanics including grapple traversal and sliding combat.
  • Built gameplay behaviors using C++ systems exposed to Blueprint for rapid iteration.
  • Designed a level centered around a growing parasite Hive simulation integrated into the building structure.
  • Collaborated with artists and producers to align gameplay mechanics with visual direction and milestone goals.

How

Combat Design + Gameplay Engineering

I designed and implemented the player's core movement and combat systems, focusing on mobility and responsiveness in tight indoor environments.

  • Implemented gameplay behaviors such as grapple traversal, sliding movement, and aim-assist targeting.
  • Integrated player input, animation states, and physics interactions through C++ gameplay components and Blueprint interfaces.
  • Exposed key combat parameters to designers through Blueprint to enable rapid tuning of movement feel and combat pacing.
  • Worked closely with artists and producers to ensure mechanics supported the intended combat rhythm and player flow.

Level Design — Hive Simulation

I designed a level centered around a parasitic Hive structure growing inside a building, creating traversal paths and combat arenas through environmental transformation.

  • The central Hive structure grows into the building while cables from the structure attempt to strangle it.
  • At cable contact points, the Hive generates hexagonal platforms that players can traverse.
  • Entrances into the Hive are torn open by structural cables, creating layered vertical gameplay spaces.
  • A critical injection point forms around a cable that pierces the Hive wall, granting players the grapple ability.

This level structure links narrative worldbuilding with traversal mechanics, turning the parasite's growth into both a visual motif and a gameplay system.
