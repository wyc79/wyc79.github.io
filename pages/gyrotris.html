What做了什么

Gyrotris is a sci-fi puzzle game that reimagines classic Tetris through rotating boards, dynamic gravity, and environmental hazards. The game is set on a spaceship trapped by a black hole, where the player clears unstable cargo while adapting to shifting conditions.Gyrotris 是一款科幻解谜游戏，借助棋盘旋转、动态重力和环境危险，重新演绎了经典的 Tetris。游戏设定在一艘被黑洞困住的飞船上，玩家一边清理不稳定的货物，一边适应不断变化的环境。

Developed entirely by me for Untitled Game Jam #100, this was my first complete shipped game. I handled the design, programming, and pixel art.这款游戏由我独自为 Untitled Game Jam #100 开发，是我第一款完整发布的作品。设计、编程和像素美术均由我一人完成。

How如何实现

Puzzle Design解谜设计

The core design goal was to keep the readability of Tetris while adding new systems that force the player to rethink space, timing, and recovery.核心设计目标是保留 Tetris 的易读性，同时加入新的系统，迫使玩家重新思考空间、时机与补救。

  • Built classic falling-block movement and rotation as the foundation.
  • 以经典的方块下落移动与旋转作为基础。
  • Added periodic board rotation, which changes how players read and plan the grid.
  • 加入了周期性的棋盘旋转，改变了玩家阅读和规划网格的方式。
  • Used dynamic hazards such as holes and debris to disrupt standard settling behavior.
  • 利用空洞、碎片等动态危险，打破常规的方块堆叠方式。
  • Included a time-stop ability and limited repair robots to add strategic recovery options.
  • 加入了时间停止能力和数量有限的维修机器人，增加了战略性的补救选择。

Solo Development独立开发

Because this was a fully solo jam project, I focused on building a clear gameplay loop quickly and iterating on the mechanic that made the game distinctive.由于这是一个完全独立的 jam 项目，我专注于快速搭建清晰的玩法循环，并不断打磨让游戏与众不同的机制。

  • Implemented the full game in Godot 4.3 using GDScript.
  • 使用 GDScript 在 Godot 4.3 中实现了整款游戏。
  • Created the game's pixel art assets in Piskel.
  • 在 Piskel 中制作了游戏的像素美术资源。
  • Balanced scope to ship a complete prototype rather than overbuild presentation features.
  • 权衡了开发范围，选择发布一个完整的原型，而非过度堆砌表现层的功能。
  • Used the project to explore how established game formulas can be transformed into new experiences.
  • 借这个项目探索如何将成熟的游戏套路转化为全新的体验。

Iteration迭代

Gyrotris also gave me an early foundation in iterative design: identifying which mechanic was novel, what players found confusing, and what should be improved in future versions.Gyrotris 也让我打下了迭代设计的早期基础：判断哪种机制真正新颖、玩家在哪里感到困惑，以及未来版本应改进之处。

  • Evaluated clarity around board rotation feedback and readability.
  • 评估了棋盘旋转反馈的清晰度与易读性。
  • Considered future improvements such as stronger rotation animation and clearer hole behavior.
  • 考虑了未来的改进，例如更强的旋转动画和更清晰的空洞表现。
  • Used playtesting feedback to think about how much chaos helps the game versus hurts readability.
  • 借助玩法测试的反馈，思考多少混乱程度有助于游戏，又在多大程度上损害易读性。
