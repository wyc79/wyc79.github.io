Codebreaker — Gameplay Systems

Role: Gameplay Engineer · Systems Designer
Tools: Unity 6, C#

What

Codebreaker is a systems-driven action game developed in Unity, focused on combat interactions and player decision-making in structured environments.

I served as the primary gameplay engineer for the project, implementing the core gameplay architecture and combat systems while working with production and audio teams to integrate game features into milestone builds.

The goal of the project was to design gameplay systems that support clear player feedback, responsive controls, and consistent combat pacing.

Main Achievements

  • Implemented core gameplay systems including combat mechanics, player interactions, and environmental logic.
  • Designed gameplay architecture supporting modular systems and clear event-driven interactions.
  • Integrated animation, audio triggers, and player feedback into cohesive gameplay loops.
  • Coordinated with production and audio teams to deliver playable milestone builds.

How

Gameplay Systems Architecture

I designed the core gameplay systems that drive player interactions and combat behavior. These systems manage player actions, environmental responses, and gameplay feedback.

  • Implemented gameplay logic for player combat actions and interaction mechanics.
  • Designed modular gameplay scripts to support flexible feature development and iteration.
  • Integrated player input, animations, and gameplay feedback to ensure responsive controls.
  • Structured gameplay systems so mechanics could be extended without breaking existing systems.

Game Feel and Player Feedback

A major focus of the project was ensuring that player actions produce clear and immediate feedback.

  • Integrated animation events and audio triggers to reinforce gameplay actions.
  • Implemented feedback systems that communicate combat outcomes and player interactions.
  • Worked with audio and production teams to ensure gameplay events triggered correct audiovisual responses.

Level Interaction Design

Gameplay spaces were designed to support combat pacing and interaction opportunities.

  • Designed level interactions that encourage player movement and tactical decisions.
  • Implemented environmental interaction systems connected to gameplay events.
  • Ensured gameplay systems supported designers iterating on level flow and player challenges.
