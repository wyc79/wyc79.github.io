Role: Gameplay Engineer · Systems Designer职责： 玩法工程师 · 系统设计师

What做了什么

Codebreaker is a systems-driven action game developed in Unity, focused on combat interactions and player decision-making in structured environments.Codebreaker 是一款基于系统设计、使用 Unity 开发的动作游戏，聚焦于战斗互动，以及玩家在结构化场景中的决策。

I served as the primary gameplay engineer for the project, implementing the core gameplay architecture and combat systems while working with production and audio teams to integrate game features into milestone builds.我是该项目的主要玩法工程师，负责实现核心玩法架构与战斗系统，并与制作和音频团队协作，将各项功能整合进里程碑版本。

The goal of the project was to design gameplay systems that support clear player feedback, responsive controls, and consistent combat pacing.项目的目标是设计一套玩法系统，以支持清晰的玩家反馈、灵敏的操作手感，以及稳定的战斗节奏。

  • Designed gameplay architecture supporting modular systems and clear event-driven interactions.

How如何实现

Gameplay Systems Architecture玩法系统架构

I designed the core gameplay systems that drive player interactions and combat behavior. These systems manage player actions, environmental responses, and gameplay feedback.我设计了驱动玩家互动与战斗行为的核心玩法系统。这些系统负责管理玩家动作、场景响应与玩法反馈。

  • Implemented gameplay logic for player combat actions and interaction mechanics.
  • 实现了玩家战斗动作与互动机制的玩法逻辑。
  • Designed modular gameplay scripts to support flexible feature development and iteration.
  • 设计了模块化的玩法脚本，以支持灵活的功能开发与迭代。
  • Integrated player input, animations, and gameplay feedback to ensure responsive controls.
  • 整合了玩家输入、动画与玩法反馈，以确保操作灵敏。

  • 与音频和制作团队协作，确保玩法事件触发正确的视听反馈。
